How It Works
01
Paste Your Script
Paste any Lua 5.1 or LuaU script and pick how aggressively to obfuscate it.
02
Prometheus Runs
The real Prometheus engine parses your script into an AST, applies obfuscation passes, and unparses back to Lua.
03
Copy & Use
The obfuscated output is functionally identical to your original — copy it and execute in any compatible executor.
04
VM Preset
The Vmify preset compiles your script into custom Lua bytecode, run by a generated VM — the hardest to reverse.
